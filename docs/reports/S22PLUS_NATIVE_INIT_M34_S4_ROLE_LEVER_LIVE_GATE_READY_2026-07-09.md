## Scope

This unit turns the stock-kernel answer into a guarded S4 live-gate helper.
S3 survived final `UDC=a600000.dwc3` bind but produced no host ACM endpoint.
The read-only stock comparison showed why the old role path was wrong:

```text
/sys/class/usb_role/                       empty/no-op on this device
/sys/devices/platform/soc/a600000.ssusb/mode   peripheral
/sys/devices/platform/soc/a600000.ssusb/speed  high-speed
```

S4 therefore keeps the S3 configfs and final UDC bind sequence, removes the
dead `/sys/class/usb_role` runtime write, and writes the stock-kernel-proven
`ssusb` speed/mode levers before UDC bind.
